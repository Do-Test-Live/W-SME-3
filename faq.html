Return policy

  • What types of bags does your company specialize in?

    We specialize in exporting a wide range of jute and leather bags, including but not limited to tote bags, handbags, backpacks, messenger bags, and eco-friendly promotional bags.

  • Are your bags eco-friendly?

    Yes, our jute bags are made from natural and biodegradable jute fibers, making them an environmentally friendly choice.

    Additionally, we offer leather bags crafted from ethically sourced materials, ensuring sustainability in our products.

  • Can we customize the bags according to our brand specifications?

    Absolutely! We offer customization services to meet your specific brand requirements. From design and color to logo placement, we work closely with our clients to create unique and branded bags that align with their vision.

  • What is the minimum order quantity for customized bags?

    The minimum order quantity for customized bags varies depending on the specific design and requirements. Please contact our sales team for detailed information and to discuss your customization needs.

  • How do you ensure the quality of your bags?

    We are committed to delivering high-quality products. Our bags undergo rigorous quality control checks at every stage of production.

    We source premium materials and work with skilled artisans to ensure that our bags meet international quality standards.

  • What is the typical lead time for orders?

    The lead time for orders depends on the quantity and complexity of the customization. Generally, it ranges from 4 to 8 weeks. We strive to provide accurate delivery timelines and keep our clients informed throughout the production process.

  • Do you ship internationally?

    Yes, we have experience in international shipping. We work with reliable logistics partners to ensure timely and secure delivery of our products to our clients worldwide.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods, including bank transfers, credit cards, and other secure online payment options. Our sales team will provide detailed information on payment procedures when you place an order.

  • Can I request a sample before placing a bulk order?

    Yes, we encourage our clients to request samples to evaluate the quality and design of our bags. Please contact our sales team to discuss sample availability and shipping details.

  • How do I get in touch with your customer support?

    You can reach our customer support team through the "Contact Us" section on our website or by emailing contact@frogbid.com . Our team is dedicated to providing prompt and helpful assistance for any inquiries or concerns.

  • Our company uses natural leather sourced from Leather Working Group (LWG) certified suppliers and other verified sources.
